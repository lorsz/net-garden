This cinema recommendation hub.

There's always a choice, when it comes to consuming cinematic story. Find the right choice. Find your next watch. Read about themes, theories, critiques, and interpretations of movies, TV series, and specials that you've already watched. For the age and art of cinematic myth is upon us.

Choose by genre, and not just the basic genres. There is SO much out there that doesn't fit into standardized old-school genre labels. Looking for symbolism, motif similarities, or other linked patterns among your favorite screen stories? Find it here.

One can browse by type of media; TV series, limited series, independent/arthouse film, classic/retro movies, or newly released (past 5 years). Find even more ways to categorize your search, under the tags page.

Searching online for a recommendation is a headache these days. Too many AI generated websites, not written by people, just oozing with SEO and clickbait grabs for your data and eyeballs. All whilst not giving you what you want.
